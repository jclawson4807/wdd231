Request Research Help

Though our volunteers are limited in how many research projects they can work on at any given time, we are happy to assist you with difficult research problems. Please use the form below to tell us about the research subject you are having difficulty finding. Each user may only have one active research request at a time, and users are limited to two requests per year at this time. Submissions are processed in the order received. We will review your submission and respond as quickly as possible.

When entering place names, please be as specific as possible. If you know a full street address, please enter that. If you know the country, state, and city, please enter that information in that order. If you know additional information, such as a hospital name, enter that after the address.

Please note the Other Sources text area. Please let us know about any other sources you have for this individual. Also let us know if you have searched for sources you have been unable to find.
